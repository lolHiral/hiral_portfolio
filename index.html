About Me

I am currently learning Python for Data Science, C++, Java as part of my coursework and exploring how computational tools can be used to solve real-world problems. Alongside this, I am also learning Web Development with a tech stack that includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and molecular docking. I enjoy building interactive projects on the web.

I am involved in academic research and am currently working on a research paper with my professor in a biological sciences related field, which has sparked my interest in bioinformatics and interdisciplinary research.

Beyond technology, I have a deep interest in literature and writing. I have read more than a 100 books and have also written and published "The Anatomy of Almost", a poetry collection which is available on Amazon worldwide.

I enjoy taking up leadership roles and have previously served as the Vice Head Girl of my school. This is an experience that has helped me develop strong communication and organisational skills.

Apart from academics, I enjoy photography and have some experience with social media management, which allows me to combine creativity with digital platforms.

Overall, I’m a curious learner who enjoys exploring ideas across technology, science, and literature, and I’m always looking for opportunities that help me grow, build meaningful projects, and contribute.
